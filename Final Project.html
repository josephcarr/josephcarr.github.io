Final Project: Bad Bunny

The program below is the Final Project for CSC106. My partner and I created a game called Bad Bunny. We programmed it in the Khan Environment using JavaScript.

In the game, use the mouse to navigate through the game. Click the "Start Game" button to play. The object of Bad Bunny is to keep all the bunnnies away from the Carrot at the center of the screen. Click the bunnies as they come into view to make them go away. There is a timer and level display at the top. Every 10 seconds the level of difficulty increases, so watch out! If one of the bunnies eats the carrot, you lose.
